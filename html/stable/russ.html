Adjective
Abstract The Russian adjectives are divided into qualitative and relative adjectives (which mostly are scaled). e.g. "sinij", "mashinnyj". Russian adjectives agree with the nouns they modify in gender, number, and case. (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_grammar#Pronouns 10.10.06) The function of an adjective in the sentence may be attributive or predicative. (Pulkina 1984 "A short russian reference grammar", p.176)
