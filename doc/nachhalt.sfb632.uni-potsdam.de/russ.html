                                                                                                                                                                  Adjective

                                                                                                                                                                  Comment:

                                                                                                                                                                  The Russian adjectives are divided into qualitative and relative adjectives (which mostly are scaled).
                                                                                                                                                                  e.g. "sinij", "mashinnyj".

                                                                                                                                                                  Russian adjectives agree with the nouns they modify in gender, number, and case. (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russian_grammar#Pronouns 10.10.06)

                                                                                                                                                                  The function of an adjective in the sentence may be attributive or predicative.
                                                                                                                                                                  (Pulkina 1984 "A short russian reference grammar", p.176)

                                                                                                                                                                              Participle

                                                                                                                                                                              Comment:

                                                                                                                                                                              The participle has properties from verbs and from adjectives.
                                                                                                                                                                              There are four types of participle in Russian.
                                                                                                                                                                              The partizip present aktive is formed from the uncomplete verbal form with the insertion of the suffixes -ющ-/-ущ- bzw. -ящ-/-ащ-.
                                                                                                                                                                              The participle present passive is built by the insertion of -ем-/-им-/-ом-.
                                                                                                                                                                              The participle past aktive is generated by the insertion of -вш-/-ш-.
                                                                                                                                                                              The participle past passive is generated by the insertion of -нн-, bzw. -енн-/-ённ- oder -т-. This can also appear in a short form.
                                                                                                                                                                              Participles are inflected in person, number, case and genus.
                                                                                                                                                                              (in according to http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Russische_Grammatik 15.12.06)
