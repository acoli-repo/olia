Annotations (1)

  • comment: "The superlative relates any number of objects such that one exceeds all the others. This is specifically the relative superlative, such as that expressed by English -est. Another type of superlative, the absolute superlative, expresses a meaning like "very" or "to a great extent," and is used in Latin (among other languages). (Sylak-Glassman 2016, p.20)" (en)
