---
layout: relation
title:  'amod'
shortdef : 'adjectival modifier'
---

An adjectival modifier of an NP is any adjectival phrase that serves
to modify the meaning of the NP.

~~~ sdparse
Sam eats red meat
amod(meat, red)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a 3 million dollar loan
amod(loan, dollar)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Sam took out  a $ 3 million loan
amod(loan, $)
~~~
