### Definition

A symbol is a word-like entity that differs from ordinary words by
form, function, or both.

### Examples

* _$, %, §, ©_
* _+, −, ×, ÷, =, <, >_
* :), ♥‿♥, 😝
* _john.doe@universal.org, http://universaldependencies.github.io/docs/, 1-800-COMPANY_
