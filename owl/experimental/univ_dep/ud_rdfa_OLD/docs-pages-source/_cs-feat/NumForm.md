Feature of cardinal and ordinal numbers. Is the number expressed by
digits or as a word?

### Word: number expressed as word

#### Examples

* _jeden_ “one”, _dva_ “two”, _tři_ “three”

### Digit: number expressed using digits

#### Examples

* _1, 2, 3_

### Roman: roman numeral

#### Examples

* _I, II, III_
