In English, `Gender` is marked only on 3rd-person personal (pronouns)[en-pos/PRON].

### `Masc`: masculine gender

* _he, his, him, himself_

### `Fem`: feminine gender

* _she, her, hers, herself_

### `Neut`: neuter gender

* _it, its, itself_
