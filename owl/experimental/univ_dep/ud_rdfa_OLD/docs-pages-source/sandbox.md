## Right-to-left text (Hebrew)

~~~ sdparse
Aדני/NOUN Aראה/VERB Aסרט/NOUN
nsubj(Aראה, Aדני)
dobj(Aראה, Aסרט)
~~~

Same without "A" (should trigger auto-insertion of "ˑ")

~~~ sdparse
דני/NOUN ראה/VERB סרט/NOUN
nsubj(ראה, דני)
dobj(ראה, סרט)
~~~
